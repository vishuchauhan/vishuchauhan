## Featured Architecture & Research

### 1. [Filtering Microstructural Toxicity (Spence-Harsanyi Signaling)](https://github.com/vishuchauhan/microstructural-toxicity-filter)
A "Unified Macro-Micro" quantitative architecture combining an unsupervised Hidden Markov Model (HMM) regime router with a Game-Theoretic microstructural veto layer. Proved that benchmarking Relative Volume-Weighted Spread (RVWS) against institutional cost proxies successfully filters toxic retail meme-stock anomalies, achieving a Sortino Ratio of 5.11 and mitigating historic drawdowns.

### 2. [Game-Theoretic Options Trading Engine](https://github.com/vishuchauhan/Game-Theoretic-Options-Trading-Engine)
An options trading engine utilizing an LLM-based multi-agent debate framework. Simulates real-world trading floor decision-making by forcing AI agents to debate regime detection, payoff visualization, and game-theoretic market dynamics before capital deployment.

### 3. [Alpha Discovery Engine](https://github.com/vishuchauhan/alpha-discovery-engine)
A multi-layer quantitative trading engine that strips away market noise using Kalman filters. Integrates statistical arbitrage and machine learning to create a transparent, explainable pipeline for alpha discovery and automated capital allocation.
